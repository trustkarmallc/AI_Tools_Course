Course Overview

This comprehensive course provides a structured learning path for individuals looking to understand and implement AI tools, particularly OpenClaw, CrewAI, and other emerging AI technologies.

  • Duration: 12 weeks (3‑5 hrs/week)
  • Prerequisites: Basic programming concepts (optional)
  • Target Audience: Business professionals, developers, product managers
